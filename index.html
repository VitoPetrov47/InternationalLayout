The description of

The Restaurant

The Restaurant "International" is a cultural institution for dates, business meetings, family dinners, lavish celebrations. The restaurant has a European style of the early 20th century. The prevailing dark colors of the interior, oak wood furniture. It also includes the main attributes of that time - this is a small stage in the hall with a piano, which can convey the desired atmosphere. Bar counter near the kitchen, second floor and smoking room. The restaurant can offer an extensive menu of hot dishes, light salads and Western-style desserts. The bar counter has a large and varied selection of alcohol, from wine to spirits. The bar has a professional staff member with samilie and kavist skills.
